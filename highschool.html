Luna Vista hosts 2 information sessions at local high school

December 1, 2024 Outreach

Luna Vista has once again hosted more outreach events. This time, they hosted TWO outreach events at their high school. Many students wanted Luna Vista to host an information session during their lunch period, so they did, and they received lots of attendees. During these two meetings, which were hosted for 2 different grade groups (9 and 10, 11 and 12), topics such as NASA, Artemis, SLS, Orion, and our app were discussed. Along with these, the subject of Quantum Science and Computing was explored. Many of the high school students had questions about our app, which showed that they were invested in the topic. This was another successful event hosted by Luna Vista.

"I did not know that Quantum Computing was used at NASA!" - High School Information Session Attendee
